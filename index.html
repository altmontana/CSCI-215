The Patent Troll

  • Life : 100
  • Attack : 100
  • Defense : 0
  • Special : Lawsuit!
    If enemy has less than 30 Defense, Patent Troll gets +100 Attack

A group of people or a business sometimes referred to as 'aggressive Non-Profit Entities(NPR)' solely bent on making money off of your ideas. They beat you to the patent office and use a general patent description for a product that they don't even intend to create. If your new software comes close to their description, they threaten to sue for patent infringement. You then come to a settlement with them because you don't have enough money to fight them in court. They gladly take your money.

The Privacy Creep

  • Life : 50
  • Attack : 50
  • Defense : 200
  • Special : Blackmail!
    If enemy has less than 50 Attack, Privacy Creep gets +50 Attack

A person, business, or government agency that uses digital devices to invade your privacy. Whether it's a drone, web-cam, or even digital listening device; they have the potential to see and hear your personal life. They can also obtain files from your computer. Technology has made the creeper more comfortable, no longer needing to peep from a window. They only need to exploit vulnerabilities in software or ignorance in users. Government 'authority' can often replace their need to exploit.

The Identity Thief

  • Life : 50
  • Attack : 5
  • Defense : 50
  • Special : Security Breach!
    If Identity Thief rolls a 12, Identity Thief adds enemy's battle points to his/hers

A person or criminal organization that takes advantage of weak passwords, leaked credit card information, and sensitive personal information. The thief will use this data to use your credit card and to sign-in to any of your online accounts. In a digital sense, they take your wallet where your credit card is the main target. In the digital realm they have to the ability to become you while pretending that you never existed. Your computer or online account will never know the difference.
