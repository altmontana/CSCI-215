The Patent Troll

  • Life : 100
  • Attack : 100
  • Defense : 0
  • Special : Lawsuit!
    If enemy has less than 30 Defense, Patent Troll gets +100 Attack

A single person, a group of people, or even a business solely bent on making money off of your ideas. They beat you to the patent office and use a general patent description for a product that they don't even intend to create. If your new software comes close to their description, they sue you for patent infringement.

Andrew Turner
CSCI 215 Final Project 2015
